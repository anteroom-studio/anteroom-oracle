## Overview

**ZAI Oracle** is a terminal-based geopolitical and macro market intelligence engine.

It does not just track charts.  
It watches the **world system**.

Oracle combines:

- live market data
- geopolitical developments
- war and conflict signals
- energy shocks
- political events
- crypto intelligence
- historical crisis pattern matching
- AI-assisted macro reasoning

It answers one core question:

> **Given everything happening right now, what is the most likely outcome — and what does history say about it?**
